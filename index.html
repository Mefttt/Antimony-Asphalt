Our Backstory

Antimony & Asphalt was forged in the grey spaces between urban decay and high-end industrialism. Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that the modern world doesn't need to be masked by traditional florals; it needs to be understood through its own raw chemistry. We began our journey by wandering the city at 3:00 AM, capturing the exact olfactory profile of rain hitting hot pavement and the mineral tang of cold iron cooling under a moonlit sky. This isn't just perfumery—it is a liquid documentation of the city's pulse.


What truly sets us apart is our radical approach to ISO E Super. While the rest of the industry hides this aroma-chemical as a subtle fixative in the background, we treat it as our primary architecture. This synthetic molecule provides a velvety, pheromonic cedar-wood quality that creates an invisible aura around the wearer, reacting uniquely with individual body heat to form a scent that is entirely your own. By leveraging an unprecedented concentration of iso e super, we achieve a haunting longevity and a structural depth that natural extracts simply cannot mimic. At Antimony & Asphalt, we embrace the synthetic grit that gives the urban landscape its soul.
